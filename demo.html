Ronan's Experimental Physics Engine

A 2D physics engine written entirely in javascript with no external libraries

Adjust the physical parameters of your spawned objects with the buttons below. Then drag in your desired shape from the top left. The engine is still unstable for bodies which have a high disparity in mass, excessive stacking and extreme scenarios, so some glitches/crashes may occur. These issues are continuously being improved and/or characterized. I hope to continue adding new features like actuators, links and even fluids, so stay tuned.
